Someone says

What just happened

You were asked to demonstrate that you heard before you could respond. That friction - that small pause - is the experiment.

On every platform you use, there is no such requirement. The reply box opens the moment you finish reading. Sometimes before.

Elie Wiesel argued that to truly witness another person is a moral act - not a technique, not a courtesy. This prototype asks: what if the architecture required it?
